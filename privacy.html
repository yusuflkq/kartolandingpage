Privacy Policy for Karto

Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers to perform services on our behalf, such as hosting services or customer support, under confidentiality agreements.

Data Security

We are committed to protecting the security of your personal information. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your information when you use our services.

Your Rights

Deletion: You may request the deletion of your account and associated data.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
